4.7 Foam Board & Forex Board Printing

Description: Rigid, lightweight signage boards ideal for indoor and outdoor displays. We mount full-colour prints on foam board (PVC foam core) or forex board (solid PVC) in thicknesses from 3 mm to 10 mm. Perfect for exhibition displays, retail signage, wayfinding, and point-of-purchase materials. Available with Hanging hardware, stand-off mounts, or easel backs as options.

Use Cases:

  • Exhibition exhibitors creating lightweight, freestanding display panels
  • Retail stores and supermarkets with hanging promotional signage and shelf talkers
  • Hotels and venues with room signage, directional boards, and event name boards
  • Corporate offices displaying mission statements, organisational charts, and wall art

Turnaround Note: Standard: 24–48 hours. Same day available for A3, A2, and A1 sizes.

CTA: Get a Quote for Foam Board & Forex Board Printing

FAQ:

  • **Q: What is the difference between foam board and forex board?** A: Foam board has a foam core between two paper or plastic facings — lightweight and economical for indoor use. Forex board is solid PVC throughout — heavier, more rigid, and weather-resistant for outdoor or long-term use.
  • **Q: Can you mount foam board prints on walls?** A: Yes. We offer several mounting options including adhesive strips, screw fittings with stand-off caps, and removable tape for temporary displays.
  • **Q: What thickness should I choose?** A: 3–5 mm for general indoor signage and displays. 5–10 mm for outdoor use, larger boards, or where additional rigidity is needed.

For precise brand colour matching, we can work with Pantone references and perform colour adjustments during prepress to ensure consistency. We recommend supplying your brand style guide or Pantone codes with your order.

Q8: What is the minimum order quantity?

A: We have no minimum order for most products. Whether you need 50 business cards or 5,000 — we can help. Some specialty items (like corporate gifts) may have minimum quantities depending on the product.

Q9: How do I prepare my artwork for printing?

Not sure about any of these? Send us your file and we'll check it during prepress.

Q10: Do you install banners and signage?
